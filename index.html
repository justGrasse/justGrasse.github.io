The Yeti Gazetti

RawwwRRR!!! Although everyone knows of the legendary Snowperson, the Yeti, very few know of the richness of the lore that surrounds him [or her, I mean it's the 21st Century after all]. It's time to take a good look at who the Yeti truly is and embrace its story justly. And there is no better place to start than... The Yeti Gazetti!
